Summary of Feedback on BOT Consultation Paper ‚ÄúRepositioning Thailand‚Äôs Financial Sector for a Sustainable Digital Economy‚Äù

The Bank of Thailand (BOT) published a consultation paper outlining a policy framework on repositioning Thailand‚Äôs financial sector for a sustainable digital economy which was open for feedback from stakeholders and the public during 1‚Äì28 February 2022.

Most of the comments agreed with the guiding principle to strike the appropriate balance between promoting innovation and managing risks amid the transition to the digital economy and sustainable growth. The three policy directions following the above principle include:

  1. Leveraging on technology and data to drive innovation through 3 Open‚Äôs (Open Competition, Open Infrastructure and Open Data).
  2. Managing the transition towards digital economy and sustainability.
  3. Shifting from stability to resiliency in terms of supervisory framework and safeguarding the financial system from emerging risks.
